# Proverbs 10:1

> A wise son brings joy to his father, but a foolish son brings grief to his mother.

<br>

**Who is the Wise?**  
A wise son listens and makes good choices. His actions bring delight and happiness to those who care for him. 
<br><br>

**And Who is Foolish?**  
A foolish son ignores advice and acts carelessly. His actions cause worry and disappointment for those who love him.
<br><br>

**Conclusion**<br>
A wise person shows good respect and responsibility, which makes others feel secure and encouraged because of his wisdom in handling daily matters. However, a fool shows little regard for guidance and responsibility, which makes others feel anxious and burdened because of the trouble his choices create.
